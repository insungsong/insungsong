## About Me

I'm a backend engineer with around 5 years of experience in startups.

I don't just build APIs.  
I work on messy operational problems where product, people, and systems collide.

My work has centered on:

- turning repetitive operations into reliable automation
- improving data consistency in distributed and legacy-heavy environments
- solving concurrency and throughput issues in real production systems
- building pragmatic architectures with **NestJS, Node.js, Redis, Kafka, MongoDB, and MySQL**
- using **AI as a thinking partner** to structure problems, compare trade-offs, and make better engineering decisions

I care less about adopting the newest technology for its own sake, and more about choosing the simplest solution that fits the team's reality, operational capacity, and long-term maintainability.

---

## What I Do Best

### 1. Operational Automation with Real Business Impact
I build backend systems that reduce manual work, remove repetitive operational burden, and make workflows reliable at scale.

### 2. Data Consistency & Concurrency
I enjoy solving race conditions, sync issues, and edge cases that appear only in real production traffic.

### 3. Event-Driven Backend Design
I design async workflows with queues and consumer isolation so latency-sensitive paths stay stable under load.

### 4. Product-Minded Engineering
I try to understand the field before optimizing the code.  
Good backend systems should make real work simpler for operators, not just look clean in the repository.

### 5. Legacy Modernization
I'm comfortable improving imperfect systems incrementally while keeping services stable and data trustworthy.
